The deal, in plain language.

Five tenants. One signature window. Every concession below is locked into the lease at execution and survives renewals — for the lifetime of the original Founding tenant only.
Benefit / 01

$2.00/SF off base rent — for five years.

Locked-in below-market rate on the original term. A 1,954 SF tenant saves roughly $19,540 over the five-year base term, before TI and free rent.

Benefit / 02

Three months free rent at delivery.

Move in, fit out, hire your first crew — pay your first month of rent after you're fully operating, not while you're still uncrating equipment.

Benefit / 03

$15.00/SF tenant improvement allowance.

Office build-out, paint booth, dust collection, restroom, mezzanine — design the suite around your operation and we'll fund the work up front.

Benefit / 04

First right of refusal on Phase 2.

When the second phase opens, Founding Five get first call on the largest contiguous bays, the corner suite, and any expansion neighboring their existing footprint.

Benefit / 05

Naming rights on suite signage.

Your brand on the bay door, the directory monument, and the project tenant map — at no cost. We design and fabricate to match the park's identity.

Benefit / 06

A direct line to ownership.

You won't chase a 1-800 ticket queue. Founding Five tenants get the developer's mobile, quarterly walk-throughs, and a seat on the park's tenant council.

Window closes
All five spots locked at topping out, or at the fifth signed lease — whichever comes first. After that, the rate card resets to market and concessions retire.
